HORSE ADOPTION PROGRAM

Chiefswood stables is a family owned and operated thoroughbred racing farm. Our goal is to breed quality thoroughbred horses to compete in the classic races. It is our belief that the responsibility of care for our horses extends beyond the finish line of their last career race. It is with this belief that we have developed the Chiefswood After Care Program. Our goal is to find loving lifelong adoptive homes for our horses. We do this by trying to match the right horse up with the appropriate adoptor.
