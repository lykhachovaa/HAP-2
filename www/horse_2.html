LASHBURN

Lashburn is a son of sire Rahy and dam Prospectors Band; Lashburn is a 7 year old gelding that stands 15.3 hands. He is an intelligent horse with an outgoing personality. He is a sound, elegant mover that would do well as a pleasure riding horse or hunter prospect. He is not suited to a green rider, is good with other horses and loves his treats.
